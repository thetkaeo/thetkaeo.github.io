Supports iOS 8 (most firmware.)

Compatible with all iPhones, iPod Touches and iPads running iOS 8 (including 8.3-8.4).

Muze is a clean and sleek icon theme that completely redefines your iOS experience. Using a range of stunning colors and gradients, Muze strips away the bland details to leave you with a gorgeous design you're bound to fall in love with.

Muze fully features: 215 icons, Control Center theme, Settings theme, 6 Wallpapers (for iPhone), Messages UI & other UI elements + more to come!
